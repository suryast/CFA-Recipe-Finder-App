Caramelised onion salad
http://www.taste.com.au/recipes/caramelised-onion-salad/d38f064a-df60-4bd1-8245-ff104a518804

INGREDIENTS
40g butter
2 large brown onions, halved, thinly sliced
1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
2 bunches asparagus, trimmed, halved
120g baby spinach
1 large red capsicum, chopped
1 tablespoon olive oil
100g feta, crumbled
Crusty bread, to serve

METHOD
Step 1
Melt butter in a large frying pan over low heat.
Cook onion, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes or until soft and translucent.
Add sugar and half the vinegar. 
Cook, stirring, for 5 minutes or until thick and caramelised. Transfer to a bowl.
Step 2
Add asparagus to pan.
Cook, stirring, for 2 minutes or until tender.
Step 3
Place spinach, capsicum, asparagus, oil and remaining vinegar in a bowl.
Season with pepper.
Toss to combine.
Top with onion mixture and feta.
Serve.
