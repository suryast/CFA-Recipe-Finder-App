INGREDIENTS
2 teaspoons allspice
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon sweet paprika
4 garlic cloves
1/4 cup (60ml) olive oil
1kg chicken wings, tips removed
2 tablespoons finely chopped flat-leaf parsley

METHOD
Step 1
Combine the spices, garlic and oil in a mortar and pestle with some salt and pepper and grind into a paste.
Place chicken in a bowl with the marinade and toss well to combine.
Cover and chill for at least 4 hours or overnight.
Step 2
Preheat the oven to 180Â°C and line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
Step 3
Spread the wings on the trays and bake for 25 minutes until golden and cooked through. 
Sprinkle with parsley and serve warm or cold.
