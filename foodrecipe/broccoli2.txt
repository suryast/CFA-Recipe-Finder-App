cauliflower and broccoli gratin / broccoli2
 http://www.taste.com.au/recipes/cauliflower-broccoli-gratin-2/03ce7311-5d71-4c95-b3f4-6b6d2d550c5f

 INGREDIENTS
50g butter
2 tablespoons plain flour
500ml (2 cups) milk
70g (1 cup) finely grated parmesan
Salt & freshly ground black pepper
1kg cauliflower, cut into florets
850g broccoli, cut into florets

METHOD
Step 1
Heat the butter in a small saucepan over medium heat until foaming.
Add the flour and cook, stirring, for 2 minutes or until the mixture bubbles.
Step 2
Remove from heat and gradually add milk, stirring constantly, until smooth.
Return to medium-high heat and cook, stirring, for 5 minutes or until sauce boils and thickens.
Remove from heat. Stir in one-third of the parmesan.
Season with salt and pepper.
Place non-stick baking paper on the surface of the sauce (to prevent a skin forming) and set aside.
Step 3
Cook the cauliflower and broccoli in a saucepan of salted boiling water for 7 minutes or until tender.
Drain well.
Place in a large, shallow, ovenproof dish.
Step 4
Preheat grill on high.
Pour sauce over vegetables and sprinkle with remaining parmesan.
Cook under grill, about 10cm from heat source, for 5 minutes or until brown.
Serve immediately.
